Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of events do you manage?

We manage a wide range of corporate and private events including product launches, conferences, seminars, webinars, retreats, award functions, and more.

2. Can I customize the event as per my company’s theme?

Absolutely! We offer full customization to reflect your brand identity, theme, and vision.

3. Do you handle virtual or hybrid events?

Yes, we provide complete support for virtual and hybrid events with robust technical infrastructure and content management.

4. What is the booking process?

Simply contact us with your event requirements. Our team will schedule a consultation, share a proposal, and upon confirmation, we begin the planning phase.

5. Do you offer audio/visual equipment rental?

Yes. We provide professional-grade sound systems, lighting, and LED screens tailored to your event size and needs.

6. Where are your services available?

We operate primarily across Tamil Nadu but can manage events in other regions upon request.

7. How much time do you need to plan an event?

It depends on the event scale. For corporate events, we recommend a minimum of 2-4 weeks’ notice. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines for urgent needs.

8. Are your services budget-friendly?

Yes. We tailor packages to suit various budgets without compromising on quality and professionalism.
